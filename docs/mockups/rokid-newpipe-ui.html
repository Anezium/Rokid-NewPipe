Rokid NewPipe mockup

Standalone HTML preview for the glasses surface. The black 480 x 640 frame is the app; the panel here is just browser control.

  • Swipe axis: ArrowLeft/ArrowRight, ArrowUp/ArrowDown or Tab.
  • Tap: Enter or Space.
  • Back: Escape hides the keyboard or player rail first.
  • Search: keyboard uses the same carousel keys as the Android controller.

Design choices: transparent black root, outline-only focus, no touch sliders, no comments on the video page, and a single player action rail that exposes Play, Full, Quality, Speed, Subs, Close.
